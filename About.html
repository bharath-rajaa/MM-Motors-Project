ABOUT US

MARUDAHR MISHRI MOTORS LLP known as MM Motors is the multi franchise automobile sales and service organization that is dedicated to creating life long customers. We recognize that to achieve this , we must provide exceptional automotive for our customers, and the very best work place for our team members.

We are committed, through support and service to actively improve the greater community; and will continually work to earn the respect and trust of our customers, employees and their families.

MM Motors will always be driven by fairness, the recognition of diversity and respect for the individual. Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd., a key player in India’s automotive industry, is renowned for its rugged SUVs, commercial vehicles, and electric mobility solutions. Established in 1945, the company initially assembled Willys Jeeps but later developed its own diverse vehicle lineup. Mahindra’s SUV range includes the legendary Thar, the powerful Scorpio-N, the tech-loaded XUV700, the durable Bolero, and the safety-focused XUV300.

In the electric segment, Mahindra leads with the XUV400 EV and Treo, promoting sustainable mobility. The company also excels in commercial vehicles like the Bolero Pik-Up and Supro. With a global presence across Africa, the Middle East, South America, and Europe, Mahindra continues to push innovation, owning brands like SsangYong and investing in EV technology. Focused on performance, durability, and cutting-edge advancements, Mahindra remains a trusted name in mobility and transportation.
